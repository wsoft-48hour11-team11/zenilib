"Wait just a moment," the demon's last artifact whispers as you seal it into the last chest. "Think of the power you could wield if you became my vessel. You would never be forgotten, pushed around, or pitied again!" Although tempted, you shut the lid and walk out the door, back to your life.

You nearly forget the offer by the time you are next hired, this time to put away an insufferable and uncompromising mage who had been persecuting people without magic. He seems uninterested in tempting you or even acknowledging you are inconveniencing him.
